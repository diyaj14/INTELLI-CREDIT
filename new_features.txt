### 🔴 Critical Gaps (These will lose you points with judges)

**2. No OpenCV / Image Preprocessing**
Your master plan lists OpenCV for deskewing and denoising scanned PDFs. Right now you rely on PaddleOCR's built-in handling. **Action:** Add `pdf2image` + OpenCV preprocessing pipeline before PaddleOCR. Show judges the "before/after" scan quality improvement in your demo.
